The Approach

How I Coach

Elite Knowledge

Everything I teach comes from what I learned playing at the highest levels — San Jac, Rice, the College World Series, professional ball. I'm not guessing. I'm passing down what was passed down to me by some of the best coaches in the game.

Calm Delivery

I don't yell. I don't make kids feel small. I coach the way the best coaches coached me — clear, direct, and with respect. Kids learn faster when they're not playing scared. My job is to build confidence, not break it down.

Real Development

Every session has a plan. Every player gets measurable data. Velocity. Strike percentage. Mechanic cues they can actually remember and work on. This isn't daycare with a batting cage — it's structured development with a purpose.

The Underdog Mentality

I came up through the grind — select ball in Houston, junior college, earning a spot at Rice, grinding through pro ball. I know what it takes to compete when you're not the biggest name in the room. That mentality is baked into everything I teach.

“Calm, cool, collected coaching. Push ’em to get better — don’t break ’em down.”
